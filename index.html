Info:

You can download my web apps and its dependencies here. Just click on one of the download buttons to start. You also get a bonus: SpeckEdit!

So what do I do now?

You can make a new web app out of my existing webapps, improve it (bug fixes, design changes, etc.), make it a real app (whatever platform you want!), whatever you can think of as long as you comply with the MIT License.

Notes:

In my webapp's appcache files, you may notice that the version number is quite high. Its only purpose is to update the apps after a change. (More info on appcaches in appcachefacts.info)

You may also notice that the webapp's codes are quite messy and non-standard compliant. I really don't have the time to fix those, so if you would like to contribute big in this project, you could fix (or at least lessen) those issues (either or both, it's up to you).

If you notice something else going wrong, leave a message to me at "simon_clemente at me.com" (no quote marks and replace the "at" with "@").
